    I've always loved technology. When I was a kid, I was the "go to" brother when it came to fixing the VCR or setting up the TV. I guess you could say I was a bit of a nerd, but I've always worn it as a badge of honor!
    While studying Busines Management at Northern Arizona University, I developed a passion for audio production. My hobby of playing the piano combined with the power of computers and robust softwares opened up to me an entirely new realm of possibilities - and I was hooked!
    By a stroke of grace, my passion ended up proving useful to me professionally, as my Father's media company was in need of an editor for their new podcast, Dentistry Uncensored. I was lucky enough for them to offer me the position, and I was very grateful for the opportunity, so I finished my last few classes online and began working right away! Little did I know that in addition to this job allowing me to explore new avenues of media production such as video editing and social media marketing, I would also have the immense privelage to travel all over the world; from Johannesburg, South Africa to Tokyo, Japan!
    Somewhere along the way I fell in love with my beautiful wife and had a baby, too, which is my favorite part of this story! Deciding to make a career change while my son was still young, I enrolled back in school to pursue a degree in Computer Science - and it's been an amazing experience! I am very interested to see what the future holds, and in an ever-changing industry, the possibilities are endless! Staying true to my roots, I have a keen interest in electronic media production, streaming technologies, and virtual reality applications.
